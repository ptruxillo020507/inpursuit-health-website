The Bridge — What No One Else Has
The InPursuit App × TETRA ex™

The InPursuit patient app creates a direct, continuous link between your practice and the patient’s real life. Device data, medication adherence, self-reported symptoms, engagement, and complete multi-provider records — all flowing into your dashboard in real time, without you building or staffing anything.

07
Patient-Generated Health Data

Wearables, glucose monitors, BP cuffs, activity trackers — all streaming from the patient’s app directly into your dashboard via TETRA Ex™. Continuous RPM-quality data without RPM setup costs. Supports stronger HCC documentation and richer OAP evidence.

Passive data capture — zero provider effort
08
Patient-Initiated Engagement

The app surfaces care gaps, overdue screenings, and covered preventive services directly to the patient. When a diabetic patient books their own AWV because the app told them it’s free and overdue — that’s revenue the provider never had to chase.

Flips outreach burden from provider to shared
09
Adherence & Behavioral Signals

Medication fills, lifestyle patterns from wearables, self-reported symptoms, social determinant signals. Providers see which patients are drifting before they miss appointments or end up in the ED. Earlier intervention = better outcomes = more revenue.

+5–10% outcome attainment from early intervention
10
Complete Record via TETRA Ex™

The patient’s entire longitudinal record — labs from other providers, imaging from specialists, pharmacy data across all pharmacies, claims history — flows into your view. Eliminates blind spots causing missed HCCs, duplicate testing, and incomplete risk profiles.

+8% RAF improvement from complete data
11
Patient Retention & Reduced Leakage

When patients have their full health intelligence in one app linked to your practice, they stay. Less drift to urgent care, fewer no-shows, more completed referral loops. Panel stability = sustained revenue.

5% annual churn reduction × avg patient value
12
AI Self-Management (Aegis™)

Patients use AI coaching through the app — with TETRA Aegis™ protecting their data — for personalized condition management between visits. A patient managing diabetes via AI coaching hits their A1c target more often, without additional provider touchpoints.

Better outcomes, zero incremental clinical cost
