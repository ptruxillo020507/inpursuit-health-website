Write-Back Architecture
Not just a window. A door.
Most data exchanges are read-only — they let you look at records but never update them. TETRA Ex™ is fundamentally bidirectional. It writes validated clinical data back to source systems, ensuring that every provider in a patient's network operates from the same current truth.
1
Patient visits a new specialist. TETRA Ex™ pulls complete history from every prior provider — primary care, labs, imaging, pharmacy, prior specialists.
2
Specialist completes encounter. New diagnoses, updated medications, and care plan changes are documented.
3
TETRA Ex™ writes back. Updated care information is validated and pushed back to the patient's PCP, other specialists, pharmacy systems, the patient's personal health record — and directly to their InPursuit App — automatically.
4
Every provider — and the patient — is current. No faxes. No phone calls. No "can you send those records?" The network stays synchronized in real time, and the patient sees every update in their InPursuit App the moment it happens.
What write-back enables:
Care coordination notes flow to every provider in the patient's network the moment they're finalized.
Medication changes update pharmacy records and alert the PCP simultaneously.
Remote monitoring data from wearables writes to the clinical record — closing the loop between visits.
Outcome documentation for CMS ACCESS and value-based care programs is generated and distributed automatically.
Patient-authorized updates — individuals can push self-reported data, advance directives, and consent changes back to their providers.
InPursuit App sync — every clinical update, lab result, medication change, and care plan revision flows directly to the patient's InPursuit App in real time, giving them a living, always-current health record.

Questions about TETRA Ex™
TETRA Ex™ orchestrates data retrieval across multiple national health information networks, including TEFCA-aligned Qualified Health Information Networks (QHINs), nationwide clinical data repositories, consumer health data aggregators, and real-time eligibility verification services. Our network connections include partnerships with organizations like eHealth Exchange, Particle Health, b.well Connected Health, pVerify, CommonWell Health Alliance, and others — giving us access to the broadest possible data footprint in U.S. healthcare.
TETRA Ex™ uses FHIR R4-compliant APIs and established interoperability frameworks (TEFCA, CommonWell, Carequality) to push validated clinical data back to source systems. Every write-back operation is authenticated, validated against clinical standards, and logged with full audit trails. This means care coordination notes, updated care plans, medication changes, and monitoring data flow back to every provider in the patient's network automatically — no manual intervention required.
Through the InPursuit consumer experience, individuals have granular control over their personal health exchange. They can view their complete aggregated record, grant or revoke access permissions for specific providers or systems, authorize what data flows where, and push their own data (advance directives, self-reported information, wearable data) back into clinical systems. Your data follows you — not your insurance plan, not your employer, not your provider network.
Traditional HIEs are regional, read-only data exchange networks — each covering a specific geography or health system. TETRA Ex™ sits above all of them as a bidirectional orchestration layer, aggregating data from multiple HIEs, clinical networks, claims clearinghouses, and consumer health sources simultaneously. One connection replaces dozens of individual relationships, with AI-ready normalization, built-in supervision, full write-back capability, and consumer-facing data sovereignty that no traditional HIE offers.
HIPAA compliance is foundational to how TETRA Ex™ is architected — not an afterthought. Every data query and write-back is authenticated and authorized. Every record access is logged with full audit trails. All data transmission is encrypted end-to-end. TETRA Ex™ operates under Business Associate Agreements with all network partners and is built to meet TEFCA framework requirements and SOC 2 standards.
TETRA Ex™ is designed for rapid deployment. Because we manage all upstream network connections, credentialing, and data use agreements internally, your organization connects to a single API endpoint. Typical integration timelines are measured in days to weeks, not the months or years required to establish individual network connections.
