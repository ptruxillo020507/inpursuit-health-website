The Living Ecosystem

Seamless orchestration between your
existing IT assets and whatever comes next.

TETRA works with every architecture you already have. EHR, claims engine, AI model, IoMT device — it doesn’t replace anything. It makes everything work together. Here’s how the pieces connect.

The Fabric

TETRA™ is the foundation. It ingests data from every source — EHRs, labs, claims, devices — normalizes it in real-time, and makes it available as a single, unified data layer. The fabric doesn’t move data between organizations. It makes data usable within one.

The Exchange

TETRA Ex™ is the bidirectional boundary layer. It handles ingest from external sources on the left and delivery + write-back to systems of record on the right. Data flows in, gets normalized by the fabric, then flows back out — updated records, results, care plans — through the same exchange layer.

The Governance Envelope

TETRA Aegis™ wraps everything. Every byte that flows through the fabric or the exchange passes through the governance layer — encrypted, audited, risk-scored. It’s not a feature of TETRA. It’s the perimeter that TETRA operates within.

The Hunter

TETRA Sentinel™ is the intelligence scanning inside the governance envelope. It monitors every AI interaction for hallucination, drift, injection, and PII exposure — operating from an architecturally independent model stack. The AI that watches AI.

The fabric makes data usable. The exchange makes it shareable. The governance layer makes it safe.
The sentinel makes it trustworthy.
